--
Overall Quality Score
This score represents how accurately the speech-to-text system transcribed medical content.

TER Term Error Rate

--
Measures how accurately medical terms were transcribed. Higher is better. A score below 85% may indicate dangerous drug name or dosage errors.

NER Entity Accuracy

--
Checks if medical entities (drug names, conditions, procedures) were correctly identified. Missing or wrong entities can lead to clinical errors.

CRS Context Retention

--
Measures if the overall meaning was preserved. Even if individual words are correct, context errors (like negation flips) can change the clinical meaning.
